Claims Processing Agent

Amplifying task workers who validate claims against company policies

Employee Archetype: Task Worker
Example: Insurance, Healthcare
Difficulty: Intermediate

Employee Archetype

Task workers who process tickets, claims, or requests against a set of company policies. They review submissions, validate against rules, flag exceptions, and route for appropriate action. This pattern applies across industries: insurance claims, support tickets, compliance reviews, loan applications.

How the Agent Amplifies

This reference implementation demonstrates how to build an agent that reads policy documents, extracts structured rules, and applies them to incoming claims. The agent handles the initial screening, identifies policy violations or gaps, and provides a summary with citations - allowing your team to focus on edge cases and customer interaction.

What You'll Learn

  • Document parsing and policy extraction using RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
  • Structured output generation for consistent claim assessments
  • Citation tracking to reference specific policy sections
  • Confidence scoring to flag cases that need human review
  • Integration patterns with existing ticketing systems

Tech Stack

Python OpenAI GPT-4 LangChain DuckDB FastAPI

Why This Matters

Organizations processing hundreds or thousands of claims per month can redirect their teams from mechanical review work to complex cases and customer relationships. The agent maintains consistency while your team focuses on judgment calls and exceptions.

Quote Building Agent

Amplifying sales ops workers who generate quotes from BOMs and pricing rules

Employee Archetype: Sales Operations
Example: Manufacturing, Distribution
Difficulty: Intermediate

Employee Archetype

Sales operations workers who build custom quotes using bill of materials (BOM) data, pricing tables, volume discounts, and company objectives. They translate customer requirements into detailed proposals with accurate pricing and delivery timelines.

How the Agent Amplifies

This agent demonstrates how to process natural language requests, map them to your product catalog, apply pricing rules and business objectives, and generate professional quote documents. It handles the complexity of multi-component pricing while maintaining your margin targets.

What You'll Learn

  • Natural language to structured BOM mapping
  • Dynamic pricing calculations with business rule engines
  • PDF quote generation with branded templates
  • Inventory and lead-time integration
  • Approval workflow triggers for high-value or low-margin quotes

Tech Stack

Python Anthropic Claude PostgreSQL ReportLab FastAPI

Why This Matters

Sales teams can respond to RFPs and custom quotes in hours instead of days. Your operations team stays focused on complex configurations and relationship management while the agent handles standard quote generation, ensuring consistent pricing and faster response times.

Financial Planning & Forecasting Agent

Amplifying analysts who build budgets and financial forecasts

Employee Archetype: Financial Analyst
Example: Finance, Operations
Difficulty: Advanced

Employee Archetype

Financial analysts who build budgets, create forecasts, analyze variances, and model scenarios. They combine historical data, market trends, and business assumptions to create financial plans that guide company strategy.

How the Agent Amplifies

This reference implementation shows how to build an agent that ingests financial data, applies forecasting models, generates variance analyses, and creates narrative summaries. The agent handles the mechanical work of data aggregation and calculation, freeing analysts to focus on insights and strategic recommendations.

What You'll Learn

  • Time-series analysis and forecasting with LLM-assisted interpretation
  • Variance analysis with automatic anomaly detection
  • Natural language query interface for financial data
  • Scenario modeling and what-if analysis
  • Automated narrative generation for executive summaries

Tech Stack

Python OpenAI GPT-4 Pandas DuckDB Plotly

Why This Matters

Finance teams spend weeks gathering data and building models. This agent compresses that timeline to hours, letting your analysts focus on interpretation, strategic recommendations, and scenario planning. More time for insight, less time on spreadsheet mechanics.
